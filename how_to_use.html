Step-by-Step Guides

Calculating SGPA

Semester Grade Point Average

  1. Go to Utilities → SGPA Calculator
  2. Click "+ Add Subject" for each course
  3. Enter Credits and select Grade
  4. The SGPA updates automatically in real-time
  5. Use "Export Report" to save as image

Calculating CGPA

Overall cumulative performance

  1. Switch to the CGPA Calculator tab
  2. Enter SGPA for each semester
  3. Enter Credits for that semester
  4. Add more semesters to see your growth
  5. View the GPA Growth Chart below

Grade Predictor

Target your desired grades

  1. Open the Grade Predictor tool
  2. Select your Course Type (Theory/Practical)
  3. Enter your Internal Marks (out of 50)
  4. See required End Sem marks for each grade
  5. Includes guards for special cases like 'RA'

Target Planning

What SGPA do you need next?

  1. Go to Required SGPA
  2. Enter Current CGPA & Total Credits
  3. Set your Target CGPA
  4. Enter upcoming Semester Credits
  5. See the exact SGPA needed to reach your goal

GPA Fundamentals

Learn the logic behind the numbers and why tracking them matters.

The Math Explained

SGPA Formula

Weighted average of grade points earned in a semester.

SGPA = Σ(Credits × Grade Points) / Σ(Total Credits)

CGPA Formula

Weighted average of SGPA over all completed semesters.

CGPA = Σ(SGPA × Sem Credits) / Σ(Total Credits)

Why It Matters

Placements

Most companies have minimum CGPA cutoffs (e.g., 7.0, 7.5) for recruitment.

Higher Studies

Universities require a strong CGPA for masters admissions and scholarships.

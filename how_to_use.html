Understanding the Grading System

GradeMate uses the official 10-point grading scale. Understanding this is crucial for accurate calculations.

Range of Marks Letter Grade Grade Points Qualitative Assessment
90 - 100 S 10 Outstanding
80 - 89 A+ 09 Excellent
70 - 79 A 08 Very Good
60 - 69 B+ 07 Good
55 - 59 B 06 Above Average
50 - 54 C 05 Average
45 - 49 P 04 Pass
< 45 (Theory, Theory with Practical) F 00 To reappear for end semester examination
< 50 (Practical Course, Design Project, etc.) F 00 To reappear for end semester examination
- F / AB 00 Absent for the end semester Examination
- F / RA 00 Repeat the course due to lack of minimum attendance
- F / DE 00 DETAINED (DE) 'RA' in all theory courses except Non Department Elective (NE) of a semester

Note: Semester wise indicative percentage of marks = 10 × GPA
Overall indicative percentage of marks = 10 × CGPA

Step-by-Step Calculator Guides

Calculating SGPA

Calculate your Semester Grade Point Average

  1. Go to Utilities → SGPA Calculator
  2. Click "+ Add Subject" for each course
  3. Enter Credits and select Grade
  4. Click "Calculate SGPA"
  5. Use "Export Report" to save

Calculating CGPA

Track your overall cumulative performance

  1. Switch to CGPA Calculator
  2. Enter SGPA for each semester
  3. Enter Credits for that semester
  4. Add more semesters as needed
  5. Calculate your overall CGPA

Grade Predictor

Predict what you need to achieve your target

  1. Open Grade Predictor
  2. Select Course Type
  3. Enter Internal Marks (out of 50)
  4. Check special cases if applicable
  5. See required End Sem marks for each grade

Target Planning

Plan what SGPA you need next semester

  1. Go to Required SGPA
  2. Enter Current CGPA
  3. Enter Credits Earned
  4. Set your Target CGPA
  5. See exact SGPA needed

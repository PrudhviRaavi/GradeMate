Important Notice

Educational Purpose Only

GradeMate is a student-developed tool created strictly for educational and informational purposes. This application is designed to help students estimate their academic performance based on standard grading systems.

No Official Affiliation

This tool is NOT officially affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS) or any other educational institution.

While the calculation logic and grading standards are based on publicly available academic regulations from HITS, this is an independent, unofficial project.

Accuracy & Liability

While we strive to provide accurate calculations based on standard grading formulas:

  • Results are estimates only and should not be considered official
  • Always verify your grades through official institutional channels
  • We are not responsible for any decisions made based on the results from this tool
  • Use this tool at your own discretion

Data Privacy

All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your academic data to external servers. Your information remains private and secure on your device.

Intellectual Property

The grading systems, formulas, and academic regulations referenced in this tool are the intellectual property of their respective institutions. This tool merely implements these publicly available standards for student convenience.

Use at Your Own Risk

By using GradeMate, you acknowledge that you understand this is an unofficial tool and that you will verify all important academic information through official institutional sources.
